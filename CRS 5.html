Mercedes-Benz India at a Glance


Mercedes‑Benz India pioneered the luxury car market in India. Over the years, the company has grown in scale and is the most desirable luxury car maker in India. Set up in 2009, Mercedes‑Benz India’s world-class production facility is spread over 100 acres in Chakan near Pune. Today, this facility boasts of a flexible production line that produces series cars, performance AMGs as well as Electric Vehicles. Aligned to the global Ambition 2039 goals, Mercedes-Benz India plant runs on 100% green energy. In a first for the automotive industry in India, Mercedes‑Benz pioneered the ‘direct to customer’ retail model – Retail of the Future that creates a transparent, one nation-one price guarantee for our esteemed customers. Mercedes‑Benz India also boasts of having the largest network spread amongst any luxury carmaker, with presence in 47 Indian cities with over 123 touchpoints. People are and will remain at the heart of everything that we do at Mercedes‑Benz India. Operating in a dynamic environment with global and local challenges, each one of us must have an ‘Entrepreneurial’ mind‑set in the face of an ever-changing landscape. We strongly believe in nurturing future ‘Leaders’ across the organization and hierarchy. The organizational culture, that is defined by the core principles listed below put our employees through the rigors that help build within them diverse acumen to lead the challenges of today and the future. We are united by a singular goal of creating an unrivalled customer experience through our products, customer services or engagement. Our people are the ambassadors who drive our ideology of a global luxury brand and imbibe an unwavering passion for the Three Pointed Star.
